Who we Are...

Mission

To champion, manage, promote and maintain life-long relationships between The University of Manchester and its Alumni in Uganda and beyond.

Vision

We are a dynamic association of fully-supportive Alumni members that will foster life-long connections of the Alumni with The University of Manchester

Get to know us

The University of Manchester has over 350,000 Alumni based in over 200 countries and covering working in every imaginable field. A huge number of Alumni are based in Uganda and the wider East African region. UMAAU aims to keep you connected to the University and fellow Alumni through a series of re-unions, social events and other relevant activities.

All those associated with the University, whether you live or work in Uganda and East Africa or whether you are visiting for a limited period of time, are welcome to engage with the association. We also welcome those who are still current students of the University to begin their journey with us.

If you are a prospective student, get in touch with us and we will give you all the information you require or connect you to the right people at the University as you consider your study options. Visit the Events page to check out our past and upcoming activities.
