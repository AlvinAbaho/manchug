Join us

You are invited to become a member of UMAAU by paying your annual subscription fees. All members are required to demonstrate their support towards the objectives of this association. Annual subscription shall cover a calendar year running from January to December.

Among others, members have the following rights:

(a) Eligible to attend all meetings and events organized by the association.
(b) All members have a right to vote and every member is entitled to one vote.

There are two membership categories.

Membership Categories

Ordinary Member

These are Alumni of the University of Manchester or its former Universities. They pay an annual subscription-and-membership fee of UGX 50,000.

Contact Us

Associate Member

These do not meet the criterion of an ordinary member but desire to join the association as members. They pay an annual subscription fee of UGX 100,000.
