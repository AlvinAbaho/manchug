UMAAU Get-Together;
May 19, 2018

Published on 23rd-Apr-2018

This meet-and-greet event will be held in Kampala at a venue to be communicated on May 19, 2018.

The event is meant to bring together members and others who are yet to commit to membership to meet the Interim committee, ask questions and discuss ideas on how we can collectively move forward.

At this event, the date and agenda for the General Assembly will be set.
