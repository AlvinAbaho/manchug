UMAAU Get-Together;
June 16, 2018

Published on 23rd-Apr-2018

This get-together barbecue event will be held in Kampala at Welo Inn and Tours located on Mulago Hill. The event is meant to bring together members and others who are yet to commit to membership to meet the Interim committee, ask questions and discuss ideas on how we can collectively move forward.

In addition, the event has purposely been scheduled to coincide with the Manchester Day event, an annual civic celebration event in the City of Manchester which will take place on June 17, 2018. The University of Manchester has requested Alumni groups across the world to participate and it is our pleasure to be a part of it.

At this event, the date and agenda for the annual General Assembly of the Association will be discussed. Details on forming a new substantive committee to take the Association forward will also be finalized. Kindly RSVP (Elijah 0771622540 or Robert 0777472366) for planning purposes.

More details will be provided via our social media platforms.
