The University of Manchester Collaboration Initiatives

Published on 18th-Apr-2018

Click on image for Slide-show

The University of Manchester supports developmental programs through collaborations with institutions in Uganda.

Some of the activities conducted support collaboration between the University and institutions of learning in Uganda. For example, between 20th to 23rd June 2017, Professor Andrew Masters from the School of Chemical Engineering and Analytical Science at the University of Manchester visited Kyambogo University and Makerere University to discuss collaborations in areas of teaching, research, joint project developments, staff and students exchange programs, capacity building among others.
